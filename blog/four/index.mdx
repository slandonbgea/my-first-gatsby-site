**What is the best way to sleep in the wild?**

I've only slept in the woods three times, but each time I wish that I had brought a tent. Otherwise you'll most likely
end up being cold and wet!

*Benifits of a tent include:*
* Shelter from rain
* Added warmth
* Having a "home base"
